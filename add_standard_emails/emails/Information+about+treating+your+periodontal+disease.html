We enjoyed having you in our office today. As you know, our goal is the same as yours; to help you gain and maintain a healthy smile for a healthy life.

Today we scheduled initial treatment for periodontal disease, the first stage of gum disease (also known as gingivitis). Periodontal disease is an infection in the gums, deep tissues, and bones that support your teeth. (“Periodontal” means “around the tooth.”) The bacteria in the built-up plaque on your teeth are producing poisons, or toxins, that irritate the gums, causing infection. It is similar to the irritation and infection around a splinter in your hand.  Just as the infection tries to break down the bacteria around the splinter and push it out of your body, periodontal infection tries to fight the bad bacteria in your gums and breaks down your bones and tissues that hold your teeth in place.

When you come for your appointment, we will remove plaque and tartar from your teeth that are irritating your gums.  With proper home care and regular dental visits, periodontal disease can be controlled so that it does not progress to further stages which can cause you to lose your teeth. 

Congratulations on making the right decision to treat your periodontal disease now.
