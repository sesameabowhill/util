We enjoyed your visit today in the office and appreciate the opportunity to discuss the benefits of Invisalign with you.

Invisalign uses a series of clear, removable, comfortable aligners that no one can tell you're wearing. Not only are the aligners “invisible,” they are removable, so you can eat and drink what you want, when you want.

Each set of aligners is worn for about 2 weeks and removed from time to time to eat, drink, brush, and floss. As each aligner is worn and then replaced with the next in the series, your teeth will move – little by little, week by week – until they have straightened to the final position we have prescribed.  You'll visit us about once every 6 weeks to ensure that things are progressing as planned.

At the initial visit, we will take impressions, which are used for planning your treatment with Invisalign’s 3-D computer imaging technology. At the next visit, you receive your first aligner, and can view your own virtual treatment plan and see how straight your teeth will look when treatment is complete.

The initial appointment takes less than an hour. Subsequent appointments will be only about 15 minutes.
