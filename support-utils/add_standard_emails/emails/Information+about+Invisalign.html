Congratulations on your decision today to straighten your teeth with Invisalign.

Invisalign uses a series of clear, removable, comfortable aligners that no one can tell you're wearing. Not only are the aligners “invisible,” they are removable, so you can eat and drink what you want, when you want.

You will wear each set of aligners for about 2 weeks, removing them only to eat, drink, brush, and floss. As you wear each aligner and then the next in the series, your teeth will move – little by little, week by week – until they have straightened to the final position we have prescribed. You'll visit us about once every 6 weeks to ensure that things are progressing as planned.

At your initial visit, we will take impressions which are used for planning your treatment with Invisalign’s 3-D computer imaging technology. At the next visit, when you receive your first aligner, you will be able to view your own virtual treatment plan and see how your straight teeth will look when your treatment is complete. We’ll also show you how many aligners are needed to complete your treatment.

The initial appointment takes less than an hour. Subsequent appointments will be only about 15 minutes.
